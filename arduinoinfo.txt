BLE = Bluetooth Low Energy
IOT = Internet Of Things
EEPROM = Flash memory that has higher durability and lets you write to individual bytes



ATmega328P (few more instructions than ATmega328; requires different bootloader)
    Bits: 8
    MHz: 16 
    EEPROM: 1 KB (100K writes)
    SRAM: 2 KB

ATmega328 (few less instructions than ATmega328P; requires different bootloader)
    Bits: 8
    MHz: 16 
    EEPROM: 1 KB (100K writes)
    SRAM: 2 KB

ATmega16U2 (8-bit AVR microcontroller)
    MHz: 16 
    EEPROM: 512 bytes (100K writes)
    SRAM: 512 bytes
    Flash: 16 KB (10K writes)



Arduino Uno R3 | Elegoo Uno R3
    CPU: ATmega328P
    USB Controller: ATmega16U2
    Flash: 32 KB (10K writes)
    Digital I/O Pins: 14
    PWM Digital I/O Pins: 6
    Analog Input Pins: 6

Arduino Nano
    CPU: ATmega328
    USB Controller: FT232RL
    Flash: 32 KB (10K writes)
    Digital I/O Pins: 22
    PWM Digital I/O Pins: 6
    Analog Input Pins: 8

Elegoo Nano V3.0
    CPU: ATmega328
    USB Controller: CH340
    Flash: 32 KB (10K writes)
    Digital I/O Pins: 22
    PWM Digital I/O Pins: 6
    Analog Input Pins: 8
